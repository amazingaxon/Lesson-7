There are 3 ways to give: You can give your TIME

More than anything, we need willing bodies of faithful believers to say "yes" to the command of the great commission. We desperately need people to give their time, effort, and heart to the calling to take the gospel of our Lord to the nations of the world. There is no need greater than this.

You can give your PRAYERS

James 5:16 says: "Therefore, confess your sins to one another and pray for one another, that you may be healed. The prayer of a righteous person has great power as it is working." We need your prayers. This mission of believers is fueled by the prayers of faithful saints who understand the importance of GOING. This ministry fails miserably if God is not in the center of it, so if you are unable to give your time, we ask that you would humbly support this ministry with your prayers.

And lastly, You can give FINANCIALLY.

This way of giving is mentioned last for the sole reason of encouraging you to give your time and prayers FIRST, however the act of giving financially is never overlooked or undervalued. We need funds to be able to send people abroud, educate our missionaries, and send support when our church plants are in need. Giving financially is a very simple and easy way that you can help support the mission of God from wherever you are right now.
